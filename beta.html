From roommates to college to karaoke disasters, they have had many great memories and many great... times they don't remember. Nick currently lives in Dallas, TX, where he wears a lot of denim.

Laura and Angela met on the first day of third grade, when Laura first noticed Angela's impeccable fashion taste (noteworthy even at the tender age of 8!). The two became fast friends, singing along to Savage Garden and N*SYNC, beating up boys during recess, and trading SANRIO stickers during lunch. A decade later, Laura met Tian as undergraduates at Columbia University, when they both served on the same APIA organization. Laura is proud to call both the bride and groom close friends, and cannot wait for the two lovebirds to tie the knot!

Today, Laura is a brand marketer based in NYC. She currently lives downtown with her husband Benny.

Bardhi and Tian met while working together at BlackRock. They sat next to each other for a good half year before having an actual conversation and finding out they both were Columbia alumni. Angela and I meet during our very first day of high school thirteen years ago as young fresh preppies. It was practically love at first sight with the most adorable little girl who turned around and smiled at me with a red scrunchie in her hair. We spent the next four years roaming the halls of prep and exploring the streets of Queens with our mischievous selves. Our bond blossomed through countless hours of ridiculous adventures; teen girl talks, sleepovers, long walks home from school that wasn’t long enough for us to finish gossiping about our day. To the years we were young, broke, and fabulously cute- it was just the beginning of the endless memories we continue to build in our friendship’s storybook. Fast-forward more then a decade later, my very dear best friend is getting married! Now that we are a little wiser with a few more dollars in our pockets, the present holds just as much significant memories to look forward to. This is an amazing and exciting chapter for Angela and I’m delighted to be a part of this milestone journey. Cheers to Tiangela and the love that brings us all together!
